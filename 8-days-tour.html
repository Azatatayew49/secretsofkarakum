8-Day Ultimate Journey Through Turkmenistan

Civilizations, Deserts & Hidden Wonders — From 1420 USD per person

Start: Ashgabat
End: Ashgabat
Days: 8
Group size: 3+
Price: From 1420 USD/pax

This 8-day journey offers an in-depth exploration of Turkmenistan’s rich contrasts, from the white-marble grandeur of Ashgabat and the dramatic flames of the Darvaza Gas Crater to the ancient mysteries of Margiana and the striking western landscapes of Yangykala Canyons. Combining archaeology, desert adventure, mountain culture, and modern city landmarks, this itinerary is designed for travelers seeking a deeper and more diverse experience of Turkmenistan.

Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Ashgabat: The White-Marble Capital

Arrival at Ashgabat International Airport, where you will be met by your guide and transferred to your hotel. Your journey begins in Ashgabat, renowned for its monumental architecture, immaculate urban planning, and striking white-marble facades. Start your city discovery with visits to the Independence Monument and the Neutrality Monument. Continue to the Alem Cultural and Entertainment Center to see the famous indoor Ferris wheel. You will also visit the Memorial Complex. In the evening, enjoy a panoramic night tour of Ashgabat. Overnight at a hotel in Ashgabat.

Distance of the day: approx. 30–50 km

Day 2 — Ashgabat – Old Nisa – Akhal-Teke Horses – Ruhy Mosque – Darvaza

After breakfast, continue exploring the cultural and historical highlights of Ashgabat and its surroundings. Begin with a visit to the National Museum of Turkmenistan. Then continue to Old Nisa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most important archaeological monuments in Central Asia. The tour continues with a visit to the Akhal-Teke horse stables — famous for their elegance, endurance, and distinctive metallic sheen. Next, visit the grand Turkmenbashi Ruhy Mosque in Gypjak. After lunch, depart Ashgabat and drive north through the Karakum Desert toward Darvaza. By evening, arrive at the world-famous Darvaza Gas Crater. Enjoy a barbecue dinner near the crater. Overnight in tents or yurt-style desert camp near Darvaza.

Distance of the day: approx. 300 km

Day 3 — Darvaza – Ashgabat

Wake up to the peaceful atmosphere of the Karakum Desert and enjoy the morning scenery around the crater in daylight. After breakfast at the camp, depart for the return drive to Ashgabat. Upon arrival, enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. Spend some time at Lenin Park. Later, visit the elegant Ertugrul Gazi Mosque. Overnight at a hotel in Ashgabat.

Distance of the day: approx. 280 km

Day 4 — Ashgabat – Mary

After breakfast, depart for Mary, one of the most important historical regions of Turkmenistan. The journey takes you across the changing landscapes of the country toward the southeastern oasis region. Upon arrival in Mary, check in at the hotel. Depending on timing, there may be an opportunity for a light orientation walk or time to rest before the following day’s archaeological exploration. Overnight at a hotel in Mary.

Distance of the day: approx. 370 km

Day 5 — Mary – Gonur Depe – Ashgabat

After breakfast, depart for one of the most extraordinary archaeological discoveries in Central Asia — Gonur Depe, the ancient center of the Bronze Age civilization of Margiana. This remarkable site reveals the remains of an advanced ancient civilization, including ceremonial areas, defensive structures, residential quarters, and traces of sophisticated urban planning. Often described as one of the “lost civilizations” of the ancient world, Gonur Depe offers a truly fascinating experience for travelers interested in archaeology and early human history. After the excursion, return to Ashgabat. Overnight at a hotel in Ashgabat.

Distance of the day: approx. 480–550 km

Day 6 — Ashgabat – Turkmenbashi – Yangykala Canyons – Serdar

In the morning, transfer to the airport and take a domestic flight to Turkmenbashi, opening a new chapter of the journey in western Turkmenistan. Upon arrival, drive toward the spectacular Yangykala Canyons — often described as the “Grand Canyon of Central Asia,” famous for its dramatic limestone formations and vivid layers of red, pink, orange, and white. Spend time enjoying the panoramic viewpoints before continuing to Serdar for the evening. Overnight at a hotel in Serdar.

Distance of the day: approx. 320–380 km by road, plus domestic flight

Day 7 — Serdar – Nohur Village – Kow Ata Underground Lake – Ashgabat

After breakfast, continue your journey toward the Kopetdag mountain region. Visit Nohur, a distinctive mountain village known for its preserved traditions, unique customs, and beautiful natural surroundings. Later, continue to Kow Ata Underground Lake, hidden inside a cave and known for its warm, mineral-rich waters. Those who wish may enjoy a swim before the final drive back to the capital. Arrive in Ashgabat in the evening. Overnight at a hotel in Ashgabat.

Distance of the day: approx. 500–560 km

Day 8 — Ashgabat Bazaar Visit – Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a visit to one of Ashgabat’s local bazaars, where you can experience a more authentic and colorful side of the city. Depending on your departure time, you may also enjoy some free time for rest or independent exploration before your transfer to the airport. Transfer to Ashgabat International Airport for departure. End of the tour.

Distance of the day: approx. 20–40 km
