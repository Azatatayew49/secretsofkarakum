About Us

Meet the team behind Turkmenistan's most authentic travel experiences.

Our Story

My name is Yakup Karakochov. I am 35 years old and I come from Turkmenistan, from the village of Bagyr, located near ancient Nisa — the historic residence of the Parthian kings. Since childhood, I have been deeply interested in the history of my homeland and always wanted more people to discover the rich heritage of Turkmenistan.

This dream began to take shape during my student years while I was studying abroad. Being far away from home made me realize even more strongly the true value of my country, its culture, history, and traditions. That was when I became seriously interested in tourism as a way to introduce people to Turkmenistan and show them the country from a genuine and meaningful perspective.

At the age of 30, I founded Secrets of Karakum. Today, The Secrets of Karakum is an actively operating company that has already found its place in the tourism market. For us, it is not about quantity, but about quality.

Why Choose Us

We do not focus on mass tourism — our main goal is to ensure that every guest traveling with us can truly experience the atmosphere of Turkmenistan, immerse themselves in its culture, and see the country from the inside.

Our company works with licensed and qualified professionals who are passionate about their work. I personally love traveling as well, and I actively guide tours myself, sharing with guests the history, traditions, and spirit of our country.

We truly believe that tourism brings people, cultures, and nations closer together. Together with our team and the local people whom guests meet along the way, we are always happy to share our warmth, openness, and positive energy.
